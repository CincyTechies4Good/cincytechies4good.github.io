Deerefield Township

Deerfield is located in the southwest corner of Warren County and supports over 40,000 people across 16 square miles. Cincy Techies for Good developed an asset mangement system that enhanced the abilities of the county to manage and account for all equipmment free of charge. This resulted in better management of their assets, provide more efficient workflow and saved the money required to purchase a proprietarty system.
